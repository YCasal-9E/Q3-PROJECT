LRT 2: Operating Hours

Basic Information on LRT 2
  • Direction: East to West
  • Length: 13.8 km
  • Westernmost Station: Recto, Manila
  • Easternmost Station: Antipolo, Rizal
  • Stations: 13
  • Order of Stations (East to West): Antipolo → Marikina-Pasig → Santolan → Katipunan → Anonas → Araneta Center Cubao → Betty-Go Belmonte → Gilmore → J. Ruiz → V. Mapa → Pureza → Legarda → Recto
  • Fun Fact: There have been plans to extend the LRT westward to have 3 new stations: Tutuban, Divisoria, and Pier 4.
